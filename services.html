§ 03 · Services

Three engagement shapes, one underlying method.

Tier 1 is the flagship assessment. Board-Level Advisory and Ongoing Integrity Oversight are standing engagements for organisations that have already established the baseline — typically following a prior Tier 1 or Tier 4 engagement.

01 · Tier 1

Exposure Assessment

One system. One scenario. A specific view of where the intervention chain breaks.

Who it is for

  • Boards and executives accountable for a specific consequential system
  • CROs, Chief Model Risk Officers, and SMCR-designated function holders in UK-regulated financial services
  • Leaders aware governance exists but uncertain whether it produces intervention

What it reveals

  • Whether signal reaches authority in time
  • Whether escalation is tested at the relevant time scale
  • Where the chain breaks before a decision is possible
  • Which controls would fail a PRA / FCA examination on §6-style criteria

What you receive

  • Attested Failure Exposure Report (nineteen sections, Board-ready)
  • Intervention viability analysis with temporal gate detail
  • Prioritised remediation recommendations
  • Direct Board / audit committee presentation

A board that cannot see where the chain breaks cannot govern what it cannot see.

From £120,000 · 8–10 weeks · one system

04 · Product

Engine Licensing

The deterministic assessment engine, deployed in your infrastructure or ours. Three tiers.

Who it is for

  • Regulated organisations running assessments continuously between engagements
  • Internal audit functions requiring a signed, reproducible diagnostic tool
  • Specialist advisory partners delivering AI governance evidence to end clients

What the licence grants

  • Production deployment of the scoring engine in the licensed environment
  • Attestation infrastructure: signing keys, manifest registry, standalone verifier
  • Regulatory library: six frameworks mapped at article level
  • Methodology regeneration from spreadsheet source of truth (drift-checked in CI)

What it does not grant

  • Modification of calibration thresholds, gate logic, or failure-mode signatures

Opinion is not reproducible. A signed computation is.

§ 04 · Engagement Model

Scoped in writing. Independent by design.

Every engagement is defined in a Master Services Agreement before work begins. Deliverables, timelines, liability, and confidentiality are agreed up front. No informal advisory outside a scoped engagement.

Written scope

MSA drafted by specialist commercial counsel. Liability capped at fees paid. Attestation liability expressly scoped to the computation, not the future behaviour of the assessed system. Five-year confidentiality as standard.

Independence

We are not affiliated with any technology vendor or compliance platform. Regulatory framework mapping is source-cited and auditable. Channel partnerships with specialist advisory firms are disclosed to the client; the assessment remains independent.

Limited concurrency

Senior advisor capacity is the binding constraint. We run a small number of engagements at any one time. Where capacity is not available we say so — we do not deliver an assessment we cannot do properly.

Professional indemnity

£5M E&O cover as standard, with attestation rider. Certificate available on request. Evidence-of-insurance language included in every MSA.
