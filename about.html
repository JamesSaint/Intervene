§ 02 · Why this exists

Governance design ends where operational reality begins. That is where our work starts.

Intervene Advisory is an independent advisory practice for high-stakes decisions on consequential AI systems. We work with leaders responsible for systems where the gap between governance design and operational reality is not academic — where the cost of discovering that gap in real time is not recoverable.

Our work begins where governance design ends: the moment control is tested against reality. This is not ethics consulting. It is not AI safety philosophy. It is the practical, attested assessment of whether your organisation can intervene in its own systems before a decision becomes irreversible.

We publish our methodology. AGDA Thesis v1.0 and Failure Mode Catalogue v1.0 are citeable works — eight falsifiable claims, forty canonical failure modes, open to criticism. The engine's output is cryptographically signed; any regulator or internal audit function can verify the computation without contacting us. Opinion is not reproducible. Signed computation is.

Intervene does not provide reassurance. We provide an accurate account of whether your organisation can act when it needs to.

§ 03 · What we stand for

Four commitments. Operationally testable.

01

Independent judgement

No technology vendor affiliations. No compliance-platform resale. No advisory-network alignment. Our interests are aligned with an accurate answer, not a reassuring one. Channel partnerships with specialist firms are disclosed in writing to every client.

02

Published methodology

The thesis, failure mode catalogue, and corpus governance are public and citeable. The calibration, weights, and scoring thresholds are trade secret. You can read how we think; the engine that applies that thinking is protected so that it produces consistent answers across clients.

03

Verifiable computation

Every verdict is signed with Ed25519 and a public verifier is available as a standalone utility. Your internal audit, board, or a regulator can verify the signature on any finding without our cooperation. This is the feature that converts "trust our judgement" into "verify the computation yourself."

04

Refusal of false control

We refuse engagements where our findings would conflict with a standing advisory relationship with the client's board. We refuse to soften findings for commercial reasons. We refuse to scope an assessment we cannot do properly. Independence is the product.

§ 04 · Where we work

Three sectors by default. Two more on invitation.

Primary

UK-regulated financial services

Banks, insurers, investment firms, clearing houses. PRA SS1/23 intervention capability, FCA SMCR function-holder evidence, DORA ICT risk mapping. The sector where the regulatory pressure and the failure-cost asymmetry are both highest.

Primary

EU high-risk AI operators

Organisations deploying AI systems classified as high-risk under the EU AI Act. Evidence for Article 9 risk management, Article 13 transparency, Article 14 human oversight, Article 72 post-market monitoring. Operationally testable, not documentary.

Primary

Critical national infrastructure

Energy, water, clearing, payments. Where a model or automated control surface operates inside a reversibility window of hours, not days, and the consequence of mis-intervention is non-local.

Invitation only

Healthcare systems

Clinical decision-support and diagnostic AI where the reversibility window is measured in minutes. Engagements taken only where a named clinical governance partner is already in place.

Invitation only

Defence & intelligence

Sovereign-capability AI systems where the operational environment and security requirements place additional constraints on engagement shape. Taken only through named sponsoring government entities.

—

Not currently served

Consumer internet · generic enterprise productivity · non-regulated B2B SaaS · advertising technology. The framework applies; the regulatory and failure-cost asymmetry is not severe enough to warrant our engagement model.

§ 05 · What we are not

Specificity is the service. So is what we refuse to do.

Not
A compliance dashboard. Drata, Vanta, Secureframe and others track documentary state well. We answer a different question.
Not
A Big-4 practice. We are a boutique. We partner with Big-4 practices where a channel arrangement is appropriate and disclosed.
Not
A policy-writing firm. We assess whether the policy executes under pressure, not whether the policy is well-drafted.
Not
A model-testing service. Credo AI, Holistic AI, Fairly AI and others do model-surface testing. We assess the control surface around the model.
Not
An implementation partner. We do not install the remediation we recommend. We hand the remediation to you, an internal programme, or a named delivery partner.
Not
An AI safety think-tank. Our work is operational, not doctrinal. The product is a signed assessment, not a position paper.
