Privacy Policy

How Intervene Advisory collects, uses, and protects personal data submitted through this website.

Who we are

Intervene Advisory is an independent advisory practice based in the United Kingdom, and the data controller for personal data collected through this website.

What we collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect the information you provide: name, email address, role, organisation, and the description of your situation. We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking tools.

How we use it

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry and to determine whether an engagement is appropriate. It is not used for marketing, shared with third parties, or retained beyond the period necessary for this purpose.

Third-party processors

Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), which acts solely as a transmission mechanism. Formspree processes your data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold. To exercise these rights, please use the contact page.

Governing law

This privacy policy complies with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. See also our GDPR statement.
