01 — Primary

The lockup.

The horizontal lockup is the default. Use this in nearly every context. The mark sits left of the wordmark with a fixed gap; do not separate, restack, or substitute typefaces.

02 — Construction

Geometry.

The mark is two opposing trapezoidal panels. Both share identical proportions, mirrored on the vertical axis. The 18% top/bottom diagonal cut is non-negotiable — it defines the silhouette.

04 — Clear Space

Breathing room.

Reserve at least one panel-width (W) of empty space on every side of the lockup. Nothing — no text, image, edge, or container border — may enter this zone.

06 — Color

Two values, no negotiation.

07 — Typography

One family, used precisely.

Inter is the system. Pair with JetBrains Mono only for technical labels, identifiers, and metadata. Tracking is tightened at display sizes; never use default tracking on headlines.
