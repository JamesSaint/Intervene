§ 02 · The window

By the time the board is told, the window has closed.

The crisis begins the moment a system fails. Authority to stop it travels upward through people who cannot act. By the time it reaches the board, the moment for low-cost intervention has passed.

T+0System event
Detection latent.
An AI-driven decision produces outputs outside expected parameters. The system runs on. No signal has reached executive level. No one with authority to act has been told.
▲ Intervention possible
T+6hOperations
Authority absent.
Deviation noticed. Damage accumulating. Signal reaches operations, but the first person in the chain lacks authority to halt the system. Escalation route unclear.
▲ Intervention possible
T+18hManagement
Window closing.
Still running. Cost compounding with every hour. Issue received at management level. Authority to act uncertain. Escalation continues upward.
◆ Window closing
T+36hBoard
Witness, not safeguard.
Uncontainable. Regulatory exposure. Reputational damage. The board is informed. The intervention window has already closed. They are not a safeguard. They are a witness.
▼ Window closed

Measurement-grade, not narrative.
